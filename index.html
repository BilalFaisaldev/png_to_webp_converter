📚 Documentation

⚡ About WebP Format

WebP is a next-generation image format developed by Google. It provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web, yielding up to 30% smaller files compared to PNGs and JPEGs without visible loss in quality.

⚙️ Settings Guide

  • Lossy (Optimized): Uses smart compression to significantly reduce file size while maintaining excellent quality. Ideal for websites and web speed.
  • Lossless (Perfect): Keeps every single pixel identical to the original image. Ideal for archiving high-fidelity images.
  • Quality Factor: Sets the compression level (1-100). We recommend 80-85% for the best quality-to-size balance.
  • Preserve Transparency: Preserves transparent alpha layers. Unchecking this converts transparent areas to a solid white background.

🔒 Privacy & Security

This tool performs 100% client-side conversion. Your images are processed using HTML5 canvas inside your browser local sandbox. Your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete confidentiality.

🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use this app offline?

A: Yes! Since all operations and user logins run client-side on your device, you can convert files and view stats offline.

Q: How do I download my history files?

A: The analytics history only stores conversion stats and metadata to save browser space. Converted images are downloaded instantly to your local computer.

Q: Is there a file size limit?

A: No! You can import as many images as you like. However, importing very large files (e.g. 50MB+) depends on your computer's RAM speed.
